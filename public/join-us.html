Position: Fertility Community Outreach & Marketing Personnel

Uzima Fertility Hub is seeking passionate, aggressive, and results-oriented Community Outreach & Marketing Personnel to help expand access to fertility and reproductive health services across Kenya.

The role involves community engagement, hospital and doctor networking, corporate outreach, awareness campaigns, patient education, lead generation, and promoting fertility services including fertility screening, semen analysis, IUI, IVF referrals, hormonal testing, and reproductive wellness programs.

Applicants should possess strong communication skills, confidence when interacting with patients and healthcare professionals, and the ability to build trust within communities.

Preferred Backgrounds

  • Medical Laboratory Technology
  • Nursing
  • Pharmacy
  • Clinical Medicine
  • Public Health
  • Pharmaceutical / Medical Representation
  • Sales & Marketing (Healthcare-related preferred)

Laboratory technologists, nurses, pharmacists, medical representatives, and healthcare-oriented marketers are highly encouraged to apply.

Key Responsibilities

  • Conduct fertility awareness and outreach campaigns
  • Build referral networks with hospitals, clinics, pharmacies and doctors
  • Identify and follow up potential fertility clients
  • Organize community education activities and activations
  • Promote Uzima Fertility Hub services physically and digitally
  • Maintain outreach reports and weekly performance targets
  • Represent the brand professionally in the community

Requirements

  • Diploma or Degree in a relevant field
  • Good communication and interpersonal skills
  • Ability to work independently and meet targets
  • Basic social media and smartphone literacy
  • Passion for reproductive health and patient support
  • Previous healthcare marketing experience is an added advantage
